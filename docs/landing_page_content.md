### 2. Mission
**Headline**: "Democratizing Access to Institutional Real Estate"
**Key Points**:
- Traditionally, only institutions and ultra-high-net-worth individuals had access to premium CRE
- Minimum investments often $1M+, long lock-up periods, zero liquidity
- Commertize uses blockchain to fractionalize ownership and create a liquid secondary market
- Now accredited investors can participate with as little as $25k

### 3. How It Works

#### For Investors:
1. **Verify** - Complete KYC/AML and accreditation
2. **Browse** - Explore vetted properties across asset classes
3. **Invest** - Purchase fractional shares (fiat or crypto)
4. **Earn** - Receive quarterly distributions
5. **Trade** - Sell on secondary market anytime

#### For Sponsors:
1. **Submit** - Upload property details and documentation
2. **Review** - Commertize conducts due diligence
3. **List** - Property goes live to 14,000+ investors
4. **Fund** - Raise capital faster than traditional methods
5. **Manage** - Automated distribution and reporting
